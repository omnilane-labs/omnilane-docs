# Protocol Overview

## Supported Chains

Omnilane is a chain-agnostic platform designed to support cross-chain swaps of native assets. Our technology allows seamless integration with multiple blockchain networks, enabling users to swap assets across different ecosystems efficiently.

We prioritize integrating new blockchains based on strategic considerations and user demand, ensuring that our platform evolves in line with the digital asset landscape. This approach allows Omnilane to offer a broad and adaptable trading environment, accommodating both established and emerging blockchains.

Our focus remains on facilitating native asset swaps, providing users with a secure and straightforward trading experience without relying on synthetic or wrapped tokens.
